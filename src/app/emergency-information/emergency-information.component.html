EMERGENCY INFORMATION (For Immediate Action)

Emergency information and instructions

In any emergency, remain calm and assess the situation. Your safety and the safety of others is the top priority.

  1. IMMEDIATE ACTIONS AND CONTACTS

    Dial the appropriate Emergency Service FIRST, if a life is in danger or a severe incident is occurring.

    1. Ambulance

      • 994 (NetOne) (Econet)
      • 992 (TelOne)

    2. Fire Department

      • 993 (NetOne) (Econet)
      • 992 (TelOne)

    3. Police

      • 995 (NetOne) (Econet)
      • 992 (TelOne)

    4. Company Security

      • 010 (Universe Security) (Company Security)

  2. WHAT TO TELL EMERGENCY SERVICES (When Calling)

    1. Your Name:
    2. Your Phone Number: (The one you are calling from)

    3. Exact Location:

      • Company Name: [Your Company Name]
      • Full Street Address: [Your Full Company Address, e.g., 123 Enterprise Road, Borrowdale, Harare]
      • Specific Building/Floor/Area: (e.g., "3rd Floor, West Wing," "Near Main Reception," "Warehouse B," "Cafeteria")
      • Key Landmarks: (e.g., "Opposite XYZ Bank," "Next to the green gate")
    4. Type of Emergency: (e.g., "Fire," "Medical Emergency," "Security Incident/Break-in")

    5. Nature of the Incident:

      • For Fire: "Smoke detected," "Flames visible," "Where is it?" "Is anyone trapped?"
      • For Medical: "Person collapsed," "Severe bleeding," "Unconscious," "Injured in fall," "What are their symptoms?"
      • For Security: "Intruder observed," "Robbery in progress," "Suspicious package."
    6. Number of People Affected/Injured:
    7. Any Known Hazards: (e.g., "Electrical hazard," "Chemical spill," "Aggressive person")
    8. Your Status: "I am safe," or "I need assistance at [location]."

    DO NOT HANG UP UNTIL THE OPERATOR TELLS YOU TO.

  3. INTERNAL EMERGENCY PROCEDURES

    • Evacuation Alarms: Know the sound of the fire alarm.
    • Muster Points: Proceed immediately to the nearest designated Muster Point.
      1. Muster Point 1 Location: ["Front car park, near the main entrance"]
      2. Muster Point 2 Location: ["Rear grass area, near the staff entrance"]
    • Fire Extinguishers: Locate the nearest fire extinguishers. Only use if trained and safe to do so.
    • First Aid Kits: Location of nearest First Aid Kits.
    • Automated External Defibrillators (AEDs): Location of AEDs.

    • Key Personnel to Notify (after calling emergency services):

      • Emergency Coordinator / Facilities Manager: [Name/Title] - [Internal Extension/Mobile]
      • Security Manager: [Name/Title] - [Internal Extension/Mobile]
      • HR Department (for staff-related incidents): [Internal Extension/Mobile]

  4. IMPORTANT NOTES

    • DO NOT re-enter the building after evacuation until told it is safe by emergency services or authorized personnel.
    • Follow instructions from Fire Wardens, First Aiders, Security, and Emergency Services.
    • Report all incidents, no matter how small, to internal management/security.
